SkyPenguinLabs - The Penguin Uprising

Born from Frustration

Starting originally as a cybersecurity branding that was going to release merchandise, fun little manuals and generic content was quickly shot down after months of operating due to the realization of something bigger than us, but something that we can take on.

Transformed From Realization

That realization was how terrible of a dumpster fire the cybersecurity industry is with content. Or really, any industry. Instead of venture capital or corporate funding that would pressure us to inflate prices, like every content creator posting courses, or alpha male wanna-be trying to gaslight you into buying their crypto coin, we built something sustainable through genuine community engagement.

These courses operate with the goal of being the most concise, compacted, yet impactful courses while also remaining the most affordable in comparison to mainstream educators which charge more, overevaluating their products.

"While we can not fix stupid, or marketing, we can contribute to the pool of solutions which aim to fix it . The first and foremost being to prioritize the investment over the return, instead of the return over the investment (ROI). Longevity is key"
